Meal Plan Option
  • Resident
  • Platinum Meal Plan - $1495.00 Dine on upper or lower campus. The upper campus dining portion of this plan includes unlimited visits to Riverview Café in Hilltop Center. You can choose any food you like, for seven days a week, plus the option of one to-go meal per weekday before 8pm (additional $4.50 deposit require for to-go container). On lower campus you will be able to choose one block meal per weekday. You will also get 50 Meal Plan dollars to spend at any campus dining location.

    Upper Campus Meal Plan - $1340.00 Dine on upper campus. With this plan you have unlimited visits to Riverview Café in Hilltop Center where you can choose any food you like, seven days a week, plus the option of one to-go meal per weekday before 8pm (additional $4.50 deposit required for to-go container). You will also have 50 Meal Plan Dollars to spend at any campus dining location.

    Lower Campus Meal Plan - $1400.00 Dine on upper or lower campus. This plan offers two meals per weekday on upper or lower campus, and weekend dining on upper campus. Monday through Friday, choose two block meals per day. Beginning Friday at 4pm and all day Saturday and Sunday until 8pm come as often as you want and eat what you want at Riverview Cafe in Hilltop Center. You will also have 50 Meal Plan dollars to spend at any campus dining location.

    Declining Balance Meal Plan - $1370.00 Dine on upper or lower campus. 1,080 meal plan dollars to use at any campus dining location. This plan is not recommended for freshmen.
  • Block Plans&Dining Dollars
  • 20 Block Meal Plan - $150.00 20 block meals. Unused block meals do not expire until your relationship with UWEC ends. Purchase meal plan in the Blugold Card Services Office.

    50/50 Meal Plan - $425.00 50 block meals and 50 Meal Plan Dollars to spend at any campus dining location. Unused block meals and meal plan dollars do not expire until your relationship with the UWEC ends. Purchase in the Blugold Card Services Office or online at e-Accounts.

    60 Block Meal Plan - $550.00 60 Block meals per semester. Block meals expire at the end of the semester. Charges will be applied to a student's tuition bill. Students with campus housing contract may sign up in the Housing Portal before meal plan sign up deadline each semester. Off-campus students need to sign up in the Blugold Card Services office in person.

    Blugold Diner's Club - Price Variable You choose the deposit amount. Dine at any campus location. Receive a 10% discount at The Dulany Inn in Davies Center or at Riverview Café in Hilltop Center. Get weekly specials on purchases at many of the Blugold Dining locations. Deposit a minimum of $100 on either of two bonus days per semester to get a 10% add-on gift card. Unused Blugold Diner's Club dollars to do not expire until your relationship with UWEC ends.
