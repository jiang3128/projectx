Davies Center: Marketplace
      Open Hours: Monday - Thursday: 7:00AM - 7:00PM, Friday: 7:00AM - 4:00PM
      The Marketplace in Davies Student Center is the main lower campus cafeteria. It sells everything from grab and go snacks to hot off the grill burgers. Explore the menu at each of the stations below.
Centenniel: Einstein Bros.Bagels
      Open Hours: Monday - Thursday: 7:30AM - 5:00PM, Friday: 7:30AM - 4:00PM
      Einstein Bros. Bagels, known for their bagels, cream cheese schmears and coffee heritage, will introduce an exceptional morning alternative that’s fast, hot, and packed with flavor. But it doesn’t stop there. Einstein Bros. also offers bistro salads, soups and sandwiches, which will be a great lunch attraction, while oversized brownies, cookies, pound cake and muffins will make for delicious anytime snacking.
Hilltop: Riverview Cafe
      Open Hours: Everyday: 7:00AM - 11:00PM
      We are proud to offer a dining program complete with signature brands and menu selections that include just about every item you can imagine. Just feast your eyes on what we have available! Featuring a wide variety of fresh food designed to satisfy everyone's appetite with food choices to rival your favorite restaurants.
